From a Manga Frame: Reading Sanae Takaichi through The Politics and Kosaku Shima

When Shohei Ohtani set a two-way record in Los Angeles that even the manga Major never rehearsed, Japan saw "a dream reaching outward." Meanwhile, in Nagatachō, another figure who stepped out of a manga frame is steadily nearing the summit of power. Reading through the "method" of The Politics and the "anxiety" of Kosaku Shima, this essay interprets the "absolute realism" that Sanae Takaichi has come to represent.
