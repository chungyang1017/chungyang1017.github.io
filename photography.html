Room III · 縁側 ENGAWA

寫真

The Veranda

PHOTOGRAPHY · 9 series · 162 frames

内と外の境。庭が一冊の本となる場所。 For me, photography is a way of looking and a way of telling. For nearly thirty years it has run beside the research — one approach reaches in through words, the other approaches through light.

The work below is grouped into nine series — Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter, Tideline, Landscape, Street, Xpan, and Silver Halide — each shaped less by deliberate project than by the rhythm of light and the seasons themselves. Together they form a slow record of looking.
