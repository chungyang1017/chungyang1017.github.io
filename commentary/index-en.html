East Asia Frontline 東亞觀察最前線 ・ A Column by Chung-Yang Yeh

A column on the East Asia that is happening now. Japan, Korea, Taiwan, and China — four societies that, in the third decade of the twenty-first century, are each redefining what "modernity" means for themselves, at their own pace.

Here I write as a sociologist, but lighter — drawing on manga, popular culture, social data, and street observation to track shifts in the spiritual undercurrent of these societies. The strict academic writing lives on the Academic page; this column is the lighter counterpart.
