葬送的福利年（金）

Challenges and Solutions of Taiwan's Pension Reform
Taiwan's Labour Insurance fund will run out by 2028 — not just a financial black hole, but a rupture in the country's intergenerational contract. This volume reframes the debate beyond the conventional, drawing on European and Japanese reforms to address generational justice, gender equality, labour-market change, and pension financialisation.
Edited by Yeh, Chen, Shih & KuTaipei: Chuliu
BOOK · 2018

轉型中的東亞福利體制

The Transforming East Asian Welfare Regimes
A comparative study of welfare-regime reform across Taiwan, Japan, Singapore, South Korea and Hong Kong. Examines why political-economic transitions led to divergent reform pathways, and rethinks the limitations of the standard "East Asian welfare regime" perspective.
Yeh, C-Y. (sole author)Hong Kong: City Univ. of HK Press
