HOTARU · FIREFLIES AT DUSK · — FRAMES

Summer is the green of daylight, and the light that rises after dark. These are the frames of a few summers, pressed between green and water-light — young maples after the rain, dewdrops on the moss, and Rurikō-in’s green mirrored on the water. Tap any one to leaf slowly through the summer.
